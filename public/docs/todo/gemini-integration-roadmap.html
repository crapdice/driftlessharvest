‚ú® Gemini AI Integration Roadmap

Overview: Leveraging multimodal LLM capabilities (Text, Image, Video) to transform the admin backend from a data entry tool into an intelligent farm management partner.

Visual & Branding

1. AI Photographic Suite

Moving beyond placeholder images to professional-grade product visuals without expensive photography.

  • Background Replacement: Instantly swap out messy barn/field backgrounds for clinical "white studio" or professional "rustic kitchen" styles.
  • Seasonal Mood Styling: Apply autumn warmth, spring freshness, or winter crispness to product images to match the current harvest season.
  • Visual Synthesis: Generate "Serving Suggestion" images. Input a photo of raw carrots; the AI generates a beautifully plated roasted carrot side dish for the store page.
Search & Discovery

2. Automated SEO & Metadata Engine

Ensure every product and box is perfectly optimized for search engines and accessibility automatically.

  • Semantic ALT Text: Automatically generate high-quality accessibility descriptions for every image uploaded.
  • Smart Categorization: LLM analyzes product names and attributes to suggest optimal store categories and search tags.
  • Schema Markup: Automatically generate JSON-LD structured data for products to enable "Rich Snippets" (price, availability) in Google Search.
Storytelling & Engagement

3. "The Farmer's Voice" Narrative Platform

Automate the bridge between the field and the customer's kitchen.

  • Narrative Expansion: Admins provide raw bullet points (e.g., "rainy week," "extra sweet corn," "first frost"); the AI drafts a poetic, engaging narrative for the home page.
  • Dynamic Recipe Cards: Analyze the contents of a seasonal box and generate a downloadable recipe card that uses the exact items in that week's delivery.
  • Newsletter Drafting: Automatically draft the "What's in the Box" section of the Resend newsletter based on the current active Box Template.
Business Intelligence

4. Admin Data Assistant

Switch from clicking buttons to asking questions.

  • Natural Language Dashboard: Ask the admin bot: "Which products performed best during the rainy weeks of June?" or "Suggest some items to bundle with our honey to increase average order value."
  • Packaging Logic: Suggest the most efficient box size for an order based on the physical dimensions/weight of products (using LLM spatial reasoning).
  • Sentiment Pulse: Automatically summarize customer feedback from order notes to identify trending requests or pain points.
