  • Automatic alerts when inventory drops below threshold

  • Integration with the existing inventory management

5 üöö Smart Delivery Route Optimizer

The Gap

Delivery logistics are manual. No tools for route planning, driver coordination, or delivery efficiency optimization.

The Enhancement

Delivery Management System:

A. Route Planning

  • Visual map of all deliveries for a given window
  • Automatic route optimization based on addresses
  • Estimated delivery times per stop
  • Printable driver manifest with:
    • Customer names, addresses, phone numbers
    • Order contents and special instructions
    • Optimized stop sequence
    • Total route distance and estimated time

B. Driver Interface

  • Mobile-friendly delivery view for drivers
  • Check off deliveries as completed
  • One-tap "mark as delivered" with timestamp
  • Photo upload for proof of delivery
  • Customer signature capture (optional)
  • Notes field for delivery issues

C. Delivery Window Management

  • Visual capacity planning: see how many orders per window
  • Suggested delivery windows based on address clusters
  • Automatic alerts when a window is near capacity
  • Ability to split large windows into multiple routes

D. Customer Delivery Preferences

  • Save delivery instructions per customer ("Leave on porch", "Ring doorbell", etc.)
  • Preferred delivery time within window
  • Access codes for gated communities
  • Special handling notes

E. Performance Tracking

  • On-time delivery rate
  • Average deliveries per hour
  • Driver efficiency metrics
  • Customer feedback per delivery
  • Failed delivery tracking and retry scheduling
Business Impact: Reduces delivery time and fuel costs, improves on-time performance, enhances customer satisfaction, scales delivery operations, and provides professional driver coordination.
