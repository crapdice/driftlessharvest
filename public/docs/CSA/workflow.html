How the CSA Works

1
Farm Setup

Farmers create a customized storefront, setting up high-conversion product pages for different CSA share types and seasonal offerings.

2
Member Sign-up

Customers create secure accounts, select their preferred share frequency and size, and complete their upfront or recurring payment.

3
Order Processing

The application manages ongoing subscriptions, generates automated packing lists (pick tickets), and facilitates seamless member communication for delivery updates.

4
Add-ons & Extras

Members have the flexibility to add extra farm products or value-added goods (meats, flowers, artisanal items) to their weekly box drafts, increasing average order value.
