CSA North Star

Our platform allows farms to manage subscriptions, process payments, offer customizable "you-choose" models, and handle add-ons like meats or flowers. By streamlining operations from signup to delivery with robust e-commerce tools, farmers can create online stores where members sign up and pay upfront for shares.

Key Benefits

Subscription Management

Easily set up recurring orders for different share sizes (small, family) and frequencies.

Customization

Supports "you-choose" models where members pick their items, or allows for add-on products like honey, eggs, or flowers.

Streamlined Operations

Manages sign-ups, payments, and member accounts in one place, reducing administrative burden.

Integrated E-commerce

Provides a professional online storefront for your farm, making it easy to sell products beyond just the CSA share.

Member Engagement

Use built-in tools or apps for automated emails, newsletters, and recipe sharing to keep members connected.

Flexible Payment

Handles upfront payments for seasonal shares and can manage gift card models.
