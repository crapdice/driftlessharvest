# Marketing Dashboard Design Concepts

I have generated 5 distinct visual concepts for the next generation of the Driftless Harvest marketing dashboard. Each design builds upon our existing "Bento" aesthetic while pushing into different stylistic directions.

````carousel
![The Command Center](/Users/zackf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17303193-4e42-47d0-977a-0fd4f012073c/command_center_mockup_1768763888476.png)
**Concept 1: The Command Center**
High-density data visualization with glowing emerald accents and neon green circuitry lines. Best for tracking minute-by-minute campaign performance.
<!-- slide -->
![Neural Growth Network](/Users/zackf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17303193-4e42-47d0-977a-0fd4f012073c/growth_neural_grid_mockup_1768763901524.png)
**Concept 2: Neural Growth Network**
A conceptual view of lead generation as an interconnected network of nodes. Focuses on the "intelligence" of the growth strategy with soft emerald gradients.
<!-- slide -->
![The Minimalist Matrix](/Users/zackf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17303193-4e42-47d0-977a-0fd4f012073c/minimalist_matrix_mockup_1768763912713.png)
**Concept 3: The Minimalist Matrix**
Ultra-clean dark mode with sophisticated typography and subtle borders. Prioritizes clarity and high-level trends with elegant, simple sparklines.
<!-- slide -->
![Bento Prism](/Users/zackf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17303193-4e42-47d0-977a-0fd4f012073c/bento_prism_mockup_1768763926763.png)
**Concept 4: Bento Prism**
Utilizes "stacked" glass layers and prismatic reflections. A highly premium, fintech-inspired look that emphasizes depth and transparency in marketing data.
<!-- slide -->
![The Harvest Pulse](/Users/zackf/.gemini/antigravity/brain/17303193-4e42-47d0-977a-0fd4f012073c/harvest_pulse_mockup_1768763941740.png)
**Concept 5: The Harvest Pulse**
Dynamic animations and pulsing indicators. Focuses on the "velocity" of your harvest, with a large centerpiece chart and live data energy.
````

### Which direction do you prefer?
Each of these can be implemented using Tailwind CSS and your existing modular dashboard architecture.
